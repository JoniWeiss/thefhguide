Choices:

A. Learn about Ohio vital records.

  1. Learn about Ohio vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Ohio Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Ohio vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Ohio birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Ohio birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.
  6. Get help with adoption topics on the Adoptees and Birth Parents Searching in Ohio Facebook page.
