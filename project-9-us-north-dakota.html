Goal 3: Learn about North Dakota vital records.


Choices:

A. Learn about North Dakota vital records.

  1. Learn about North Dakota vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the North Dakota Public Health site.
  3. Search for online North Dakota vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore North Dakota birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore North Dakota birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

B. Explore North Dakota marriage and death records.

  1. Explore links for North Dakota marriage records.
  2. Explore the Ancestors at Rest site for North Dakota death records.
  3. Explore the ObituariesHelp site for North Dakota obituaries.
  4. Explore North Dakota death records, indexes, and obituaries.

Goal 12: Get help with your North Dakota research.


Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the North Dakota Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the North Dakota Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the North Dakota State Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for North Dakota.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for North Dakota research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about North Dakota research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for North Dakota research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the North Dakota research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about North Dakota research.

C. Learn about North Dakota genealogy societies.

  1. Learn about genealogy societies in North Dakota in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
