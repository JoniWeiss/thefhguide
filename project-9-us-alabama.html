Goal 3: Learn about Alabama vital and census records.


Choices:

A Learn about Alabama vital records.

  1. Learn about Alabama vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Alabama Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Alabama vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Alabama birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Alabama birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

B Explore Alabama marriage and death records.

  1. Explore links for Alabama marriage records.
  2. Learn about Alabama divorce records in this article.
  3. Learn about Alabama obituaries in this article.
  4. Explore the Ancestors at Rest site for Alabama death records.
  5. Explore the ObituariesHelp site for Alabama obituaries.
  6. Explore Alabama death records, indexes, and obituaries.

Goal 12: Get help with your Alabama research.


Choices:

A Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Alabama Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Alabama Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the Alabama Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Alabama.

B Join a mailing list or message board for Alabama research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Alabama research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Alabama research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Alabama research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Alabama research.

C Learn about Alabama genealogy societies.

  1. Learn about genealogy societies in Alabama in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
  2. Explore Alabama genealogy society sites on Cyndi's list.
