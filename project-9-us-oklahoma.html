Choices:

A. Learn about Oklahoma vital records.

  1. Learn about Oklahoma vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Oklahoma Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Oklahoma vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Oklahoma birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Oklahoma birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

Goal 12: Get help with your Oklahoma research.


Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Oklahoma Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Oklahoma Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the Oklahoma Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Oklahoma.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Oklahoma research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Oklahoma research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Oklahoma research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Oklahoma research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Oklahoma research.

C. Learn about Oklahoma genealogy societies.

  1. Learn about genealogy societies in Oklahoma in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
