Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Minnesota Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Minnesota Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the Minnesota Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Minnesota.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Minnesota research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Minnesota research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Minnesota research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Minnesota research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Minnesota research.
