Goal 3: Learn about Arizona vital and census records.


Choices:

A. Learn about Arizona vital records.

  1. Learn about Arizona vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Arizona Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Arizona vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Arizona birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Arizona birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

B. Explore Arizona marriage and death records.

  1. Explore links for Arizona marriage records.
  2. Explore the ObituariesHelp site for Arizona obituaries.

Goal 12: Get help with your Arizona research.


Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Arizona Genealogy page on Facebook.
  2. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Arizona.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Arizona research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Arizona research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Arizona research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Arizona research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Arizona research.
