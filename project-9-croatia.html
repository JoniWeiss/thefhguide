Goal 9: Get help with your Croatia research.

  1. See the Croatian Heritage and Genealogy Facebook page.
  2. Visit the Central Europe Genealogy Research Community on Facebook.
  3. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Croatia.

  1. Search the RootsWeb site for Croatia research mailing lists.
  2. Explore the Croatia research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
