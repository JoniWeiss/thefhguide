Choices:

A. Learn about Nebraska vital records.

  1. Learn about Nebraska vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Nebraska Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Nebraska vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Nebraska birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Nebraska birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

  1. Explore links for Nebraska marriage records.

Goal 12: Get help with your Nebraska research.


Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Nebraska Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Nebraska Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the Nebraska State Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Nebraska.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Nebraska research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Nebraska research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Nebraska research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Nebraska research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Nebraska research.

C. Learn about Nebraska genealogy societies.

  1. Learn about genealogy societies in Nebraska in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
  2. Learn about genealogy societies in Nebraska in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
