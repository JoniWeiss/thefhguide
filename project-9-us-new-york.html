  1. Learn about New York vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the New_York Public Health site.
  3. Search for online New York vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore New York birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore New York birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.
  6. Get help with adoption topics on the New York Adoptees Facebook page.

    1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about New York research.
