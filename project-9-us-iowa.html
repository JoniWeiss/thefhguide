Choices:

A. Learn about Iowa vital records.

  1. Learn about Iowa vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Iowa Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Iowa vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Iowa birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Iowa birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

Goal 12: Get help with your Iowa research.


Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Iowa Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Iowa Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. Visit the Iowa Genealogical Society page on Facebook.
  4. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Iowa.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Iowa research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Iowa research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Iowa research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Iowa research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Iowa research.

C. Learn about Iowa genealogy societies.

  1. Learn about genealogy societies in Iowa in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
  2. Learn about genealogy societies in Iowa in this FamilySearch Wiki article.
