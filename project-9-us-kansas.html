  1. Learn about Kansas vital records in this article.
  2. Explore links for vital records on the Kansas Public Health site.
  3. Search for online Kansas vital records and find addresses for state offices to write to for records.
  4. Explore Kansas birth, marriage, and death records on the AccessGenealogy site.
  5. Explore Kansas birth certificate records on the Birth-Certificates.net site.

Choices:

A. Use Facebook pages to get answers to your questions.

  1. Visit the Kansas Genealogy Network page on Facebook.
  2. Visit the Kansas Genealogy page on Facebook.
  3. See the Genealogy on Facebook list of Facebook groups dedicated to helping with research for Kansas.

B. Join a mailing list or message board for Kansas research.

  1. Join the RootsChat forum and post questions about Kansas research.
  2. Search the RootsWeb site for Kansas research mailing lists.
  3. Explore the Kansas research mailing lists on Cyndi's List.
  4. Use the RootsWeb message boards to read or post questions about Kansas research.
